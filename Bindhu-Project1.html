What is music therapy?

  • Music therapy is the clinical use of music to accomplish individualized goals
    such as reducing stress, improving mood and self-expression.

  • Music therapy experiences may include listening, singing, playing instruments, or composing music.

  • Music therapy may help you psychologically, emotionally, physically, spiritually, cognitively and socially.

Benefits of music therapy

  • Lowering blood pressure.

  • Improving memory.

  • Reducing muscle tension.

Do music therapists work with children and adolescents?

Yes. Music therapy may help with the following:

  • Behavior disorders.

  • Attention deficit/Hyperactivity disorder (ADHD).

  • Trauma.
